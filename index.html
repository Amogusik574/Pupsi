Levels

A Level is a location the Player can explore and must escape to move onto the next one. Every Level is different in a unique way shape or form, with some having no dangerous Entities, and some being full of extremely dangerous entities. No two Levels will be the same, with any similar Levels having a slight change to it.

Currently, there are:

32 Levels in the Game

9 Sub-Levels in the Game

6 Upcoming Levels planned to be added to the Game

1 Scrapped Level that won't be added to the Game

Entities

Entities are Monsters found throughout Escape the Backrooms. Most Entities are aggressive towards the player and will try to kill them, while others are friendly. All Entities are heavily different in appearance, mechanics, and habitats. Entities can be found on a variety of different Levels, meaning some Entities can be found on different Levels than others. There are currently 21 Entities in the Game, 1 Upcoming Entity, and 3 Scrapped Entities.

All Entities:

Player, Howler, Skin-Stealer, Smiler, Lighthead, Hound, Male Deathmoth, Female Deathmoth, Partygoer, Hydrolitis Plague, Scratcher, Animations, The Animated King, Wretch, The Thing on Level 7, Moai Statue, The Neighborhood Watch, Jerry, Faceling, Bone Thief, Clump, Aranea Membri, Windows, Party Host, Partypooper, Kitty.

Items

Items are Objects found throughout Escape the Backrooms. They can be stored in The Player's Inventory and can be used. Items can be all sorts of things, ranging from Almond Water to LiDAR Scanners. There are currently 20 Items in the Game, and 1 Upcoming Item.

Currently, there are:

Almond Water, Juice, Energy Bar, Liquid Pain, Flashlight, Walkie Talkie, Crowbar, Chainsaw, Rope, LiDAR Scanner, Almond Concentrate, Bug Spray, Thermometer, Moth Jelly, Flare Gun, Diving Helmet, Ticket, Glowstick, Firework, Camera
